Pressure Fields

Fields

Atmosphere An atmosphere (symbol: atm) is a unit of pressure equals to 101325 pascals or 1.01325 bars.
Bar A bar (symbol: bar) is a unit of pressure equals to a hundred thousand (10^5) pascals.
HectoPascal An hectopascal (symbol: hPa) is a unit of pressure equals to one hundred (10^2) pascals.
KiloPascal A kilopascal (symbol: kPa) is a unit of pressure equals to one thousand (10^3) pascals.
MilliBar A millibar (symbol: mbar) is a unit of pressure equals to one thousandth (1/10^3) of a bar.
Pascal A pascal (symbol: Pa) is a derived unit of pressure.

It is defined as one newton per square meter.
