Frequency Fields

Fields

Hertz A hertz (symbol: Hz) is the base metric system unit of frequency.

It is defined as one cycle per second.

KiloHertz A kilohertz (symbol: KHz) is a unit of frequency equals to one thousand (10^3) of hertzs.
MegaHertz A megahertz (symbol: MHz) is a unit of frequency equals to one million (10^6) hertzs.
RPM The revolutions per minute (symbol: rpm) is a unit of frequency of rotation, expressing the number of rotations around a fixed axis in one minute.
